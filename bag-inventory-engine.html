BAG Inventory Engine

An inventory workflow connecting Google Forms, Sheets, Drive, and Shopify-style operations patterns—built for small businesses in Beaufort and the Lowcountry.

What this lab demonstrates

  • Structured intake via Google Forms with validated fields and consistent naming.
  • Spreadsheet-first operations: reporting views, filtering, status control, and audit trails.
  • Drive organization patterns: folders, filenames, attachments, and searchable metadata.
  • Retail-style patterns applied to small teams: SKUs, categories, locations, and reorder logic.

Useful for: studios, shops, consignment ops, resale, parts tracking, and any business with “where is it / what is it / do we have it?” problems.

Hero image: Pexels.

Deliverables

  • Form + schema definition (fields, rules, and data types)
  • Sheet tabs: intake, master inventory table, reporting views
  • Drive structure + naming conventions
  • Automation hooks (notifications, file routing, status updates)
