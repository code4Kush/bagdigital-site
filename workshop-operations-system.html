DESIGN LAB 2025 SYSTEMS

Workshop Operations System

A lightweight tracking system for parts, tools, and jobs—built for small teams that need clarity without “big software.”

What this lab demonstrates

  • Parts tracking: inventory counts, reorder points, supplier references.
  • Tool accountability: maintenance dates, condition notes, and location.
  • Job tracking: status, dependencies, and deadlines.
  • Simple governance: one source of truth + clean reporting views.

Useful for: contractors, shops, crews, fabricators, installers, and any business with recurring jobs + supplies.

Hero image: Pexels.

Deliverables

  • Tracking sheet structure (parts/tools/jobs)
  • Status rules + ownership
  • Reporting dashboard views
  • Optional forms for requests and updates

Designed to reduce “where is it / who has it / what’s next?” problems.
